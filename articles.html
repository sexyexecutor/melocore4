Coping with Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

By Dr. Emily Taylor | June 5, 2024

PTSD is a mental health condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. Individuals with PTSD may relive the event through flashbacks and nightmares, leading to severe anxiety and uncontrollable thoughts about the event. Understanding PTSD and learning coping mechanisms can help manage symptoms and improve quality of life.
